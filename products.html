A Closer Look: Rome Italy Temple

The Rome Italy temple is one of the most beautiful temples in the world today. It was announced in 2008 but only recently finished construction in 2019. It has a gorgeous floor-to-ceiling stained glass wall at its entrance, an mural depicting an Italian landscape, an elliptical baptisimal font, and many other beautiful details contributing to its breathtaking beauty.

A Closer Look: Cape Town South Africa Temple

The Cape Town South Africa temple is one of two temples in South Africa and 8 total in Africa. It was announced in 2021 and will see construction in the coming years.

A Closer Look: St. Paul Minnesota Temple

The St. Paul Minnesota temple is the only temple in Minnesota and is the closest option for many members in Minnesota, Iowa, the Dakotas, and even parts of Wisconsin and Canada. It was the 69th dedicated temple when President Gordon B. Hinckley dedicated it in January of 2000.
